About Us

GrangeMobile is a useful app designed to help students and lecturers manage their academic life in a single space. The app enables users to store information such as personal events and notes securely. Additionally, it provides lecturers with the ability to update and delete student information or modules.

With the app's easy navigation, users can access the classes they need to attend on campus without any hassle. GrangeMobile is an all-in-one solution that streamlines academic life and improves the organization of educational materials.
